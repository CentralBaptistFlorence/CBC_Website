The Harbor Student Ministry

A student ministry where teenagers are loved, known, and challenged to grow as followers of Jesus.

Our desire is to help students build a faith that is truly their own. We want every teenager to know they are loved by God, welcomed into a community that cares for them, and challenged to grow as followers of Jesus.

Whether your student has grown up in church or is walking through our doors for the very first time, they'll find a place where they can belong, ask honest questions, build lasting friendships, and discover God's purpose for their lives.

We believe parents are the primary spiritual influence in a teenager's life, and we are committed to partnering with families as students navigate middle school, high school, and beyond.

What to Expect

📖 Sunday School

Students gather each Sunday for Sunday School designed specifically for middle and high school students. Through engaging teaching and meaningful discussion, we help teenagers understand God's Word and apply it to everyday life.

⚡ Wednesday Nights

Students experience energetic games, authentic worship, and relevant Bible teaching, where they can build friendships and grow deeper in their faith.

🏕️ Events & Camps

Throughout the year we offer camps, retreats, mission opportunities, service projects, and fun events that help students connect while growing closer to Christ — creating lifelong memories and encouraging students to live out their faith.

Our Leaders

Our student ministry is led by Amelia Wilson, who genuinely loves teenagers and is passionate about pointing them to Jesus. Amelia and our volunteers are committed to creating a safe, welcoming environment where students are known, encouraged, and challenged to grow spiritually.

Our Mission

Helping students know Jesus, grow in their faith, build authentic relationships, and boldly live for Christ wherever God has placed them. No matter where you are on your journey, there's a place for you at The Harbor Student Ministry. We'd love to meet you!
