Meet Our Staff

Get to know the people who serve and lead at Central Baptist Church

Todd Wilson

Pastor

Todd has been blessed to call the Shoals area home for most of his life. He and his wife, Amelia, were married in 2005 and are raising four energetic and wonderful children — Jay, Zavien, Daniel, and Madelyn.

After graduating from Lexington High School, he attended the University of North Alabama, where he had the opportunity to play football under Coach Bill Hyde. Following graduation in 2003, he began a career in home finance, working with Wells Fargo Financial before moving into banking with Regions Bank in 2006. In 2021, he joined First Southern Bank. Throughout his career, God has blessed him with incredible teams, and he is grateful for the opportunity to make a difference in people's lives each day.

From an early age, he felt God calling him to serve. While he did not always understand what that would look like, he has sought to follow Him and grow in His grace along the way. Through life's challenges, God has continued to deepen his faith and guide him in walking closely with Him.

Today, his heart is to help others build a strong foundation of faith — especially in a world that often pulls in the opposite direction. The desire of the church is to grow closer to God and to share His love with those around them, so that when life's challenges come, they can stand firm in Him.

It is truly a blessing for him to serve as Pastor and to experience the love and joy of this church family each day. He is excited about what God is doing and where He is leading.

Amelia Wilson

Youth Director

Amelia currently serves as the Youth Director. She began in youth ministry alongside her husband, Todd, and quickly discovered a deep calling to invest in the lives of students as they navigate life and faith. She has a heart for young people and a passion for helping them grow in confidence, identity, and their relationship with God. Amelia is committed to creating an environment where every student feels welcome, known, and encouraged to pursue a genuine walk with Christ.

Todd and Amelia were married in 2005 and are blessed with four children. Outside of ministry, she owns and operates a mechanic shop. In her free time, she enjoys spending time with her family, crafting, and studying.

Tiffany Wells

Kids Director

Tiffany serves as our Kids Ministry Director. She moved to Alabama from Colorado in 2001, where she later met her husband, Mark. Together, they are joyfully raising their two teenagers, Lyla and Atlas.

Mrs. Tiffany is passionate about creating a safe, nurturing environment where every child feels loved, known, and valued. She is dedicated to partnering with families to help children build a strong foundation in Christ — one that will guide them as they grow and follow Him each day.

Miles Muse

Worship Leader

Miles Muse serves as our Worship Leader. He has lived in the Shoals area his entire life and has been married to his wife, Linsey, since 2008. Together, they are blessed with two daughters, Zoey and Emma.

Miles has a deep love for worship and a heart for seeing people encounter God in a real and personal way. He believes worship is more than music — it's a lifestyle — and is passionate about leading others to grow in their faith as they honor God together. When he's not serving at church, Miles enjoys spending time with his family and investing in the people around him.
