CBC Kids

Welcome to the Central Baptist Church Kids Ministry!

At Central Baptist Church, we believe in the importance of nurturing children in their faith and helping them grow into strong, confident believers. CBC Kids is dedicated to providing a safe, engaging, and fun environment where children can learn about God's love, develop a personal relationship with Jesus, and build lasting friendships.

Our Mission

The Central Baptist Church Kids Ministry exists to guide children toward a life of faith, hope, and love. Through dynamic Bible teaching, hands-on activities, and age-appropriate lessons, we aim to instill biblical values in the hearts of children, equipping them to be the next generation of disciples.

What We Offer

📖 Bible Lessons & Activities

Our classes are designed for children from nursery age to elementary school, where they can dive into the Word of God through storytelling, crafts, games, and songs that make learning fun and memorable.

🤗 Safe & Loving Environment

Your child's well-being is our top priority. Our team of dedicated, trained, and background-checked volunteers ensures that each child feels loved and cared for in a welcoming atmosphere.

🎵 Interactive Worship

Kids are invited to participate in engaging worship services with music, prayer, and activities that foster spiritual growth and help them connect with God.

🎉 Special Events & Programs

From Vacation Bible School (VBS) to holiday celebrations, we host special events throughout the year that provide opportunities for children to learn and grow in a fun, social environment.

Why CBC Kids?

We believe that teaching children about Christ is not just about education — it's about shaping their hearts for eternity. CBC Kids is designed to encourage children in their spiritual journey while also providing a sense of community. It's a place where they can make lifelong memories, grow in their faith, and be encouraged in their relationship with Jesus.

Join Us!

We invite you to bring your children to experience the love and joy of CBC Kids at Central Baptist Church. Whether you're new to the area or looking for a church home, we would love for you and your child to become part of our family!

For more information about our programs or to get involved as a volunteer, please contact us or visit us during our service times. We can't wait to meet you!
