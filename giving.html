Giving

Your generosity makes a difference

At Central Baptist Church, we believe that giving is an act of worship and a reflection of God's generosity toward us. Your faithful contributions support our ministries, outreach efforts, and the daily operations that allow us to serve our church family and community.

Whether you give during our Sunday service or online, every gift — no matter the size — helps further God's work through Central Baptist Church. Thank you for your faithfulness and generosity.

Ways to Give

💒 In Person
