Welcome Home

We're so glad you took a moment to visit our website. At Central Baptist Church, our desire is simple — to create a place where people feel welcomed, known, and pointed to Christ. Whether you're new to the area, have lived here your whole life, or are just passing through, there's a place for you here.

As you look around, we hope you'll find helpful information about what to expect when you visit and the ways we walk alongside individuals and families through our ministries.

If you're able, we'd love for you to join us this Sunday. It would be an honor to have you as our guest and worship together. We're looking forward to meeting you.

Our Mission

Our mission is to exalt Jesus Christ as Savior and Lord and to lead all people into a life-changing, ever-growing relationship with Him. We honor Jesus Christ as our head by upholding these core values and we seek Him through prayer to engage His heart, discover His will, accomplish His purpose, and experience His power.
